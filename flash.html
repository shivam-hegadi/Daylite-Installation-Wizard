Daylite Firmware Installation

Welcome to the Daylite firmware installation wizard

Step 1: Connect Your Device

Plug your Daylite device into your computer using a USB cable.

Step 2: Install Firmware

Click the install button below and follow the prompts to select your device.

⚠️ NOTE: Make sure to close any applications using your device's COM port (e.g., Serial monitor)

⚠️ Browser Not Supported

Your browser does not support the Web Serial API. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
